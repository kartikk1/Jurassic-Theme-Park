Our main attractions

Time Traveler

Time Traveler became the tallest and fastest spinning roller coaster, and the first of its kind to feature three inversions. In 2018, the roller coaster was listed by Amusement Today's annual Golden Ticket Awards as the year's second-best new roller coaster and the 18th-best overall among steel coasters.

VR-Jurassic Roller

We have equiped certain rides of our theme park with the latest generation VR Technology . It will give you an extraordinary feel of Jurassic World while enjoyin roller coaster . We are first to adapt this technlogy . This ride has also won certain awards .

Tilt-A-Whirl

The ride consists of seven freely-spinning cars that hold four riders each, which are attached at fixed pivot points on a rotating platform. As the platform rotates, parts of the platform are raised , with the resulting centrifugal and gravitational forces on the revolving cars causing them to spin in different directions.

Velocicoaster

Riders will be propelled 155 feet into the air before plunging into an 80-degree drop. The coaster will also feature a zero-gravity inverted stall and a 360-degree barrel roll above the lagoon and riders will experience every drop, twist, and turn with just a lap bar holding them in place — there are no shoulder restraints.

Spinning Gravity Ride

The ride is completely enclosed, with 48 padded panels lining the inside wall. Riders lean against these panels, which are angled back. As the ride rotates, centrifugal force is exerted against the pads by the rider, removing the rider from the floor, due to the slant.

Universal Orlando

This is the award winning ride of our theme park . It has brought two international awards . The ride is the longest roller coaster equiped with green leds all over the path . One of the most beautiful ride in world.
